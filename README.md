# Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Objective
To analyze sales performance and profitability across regions, categories, and time periods using Excel and Power BI.

## Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning, Pivot Tables, Analysis)
- Power BI (Data Modeling, DAX, Interactive Dashboards)

## Dataset
Sample Superstore sales dataset containing order, sales, profit, region, and category details.

## Key Business Insights
- West region records the highest sales and profit margin
- Technology category contributes the maximum revenue
- Sales peak during year-end months, especially November and December
- Central region generates good revenue but has a lower average profit margin

## Dashboard Features
- KPI cards for Total Sales, Total Profit, and Profit Margin
- Sales and profit analysis by Region and Category
- Monthly sales trend analysis
- Interactive slicers for Region, Category, and Year

## Outcome
This project demonstrates my ability to clean and analyze business data, identify trends, and present insights through clear and interactive dashboards suitable for business decision-making.
